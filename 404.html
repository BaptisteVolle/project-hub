About Me

Hello, I'm Baptiste Volle, a Software Engineer with experience since 2019, following my master's degree from the Université de Montpellier. I'm passionate about software development, especially in creating web applications. Over the years, I've worked on various projects for AIRBUS :

  • myPlanning: a tool that allows users to create, edit, and share easily readable schedules for Systems development.
  • RTI Catalogue: a tool for creating and sharing Ground retest data on Aircraft.
  • NC Clustering: a web application for analyzing Aircraft Non-conformities.

Aside from my professional work, I enjoy creating small applications for personal projects. This allows me to explore new technologies and continue learning.
